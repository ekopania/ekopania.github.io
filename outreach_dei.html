Outreach and DEI

I strongly believe that it is important to diversify the scientific community by creating an inclusive environment for people of all backgrounds. Outreach is one of many ways to help create opportunities for all to participate in science. I also believe it is important to communicate the value of science to the general public.

Below is a list of some outreach and diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) organizations I have participated in.

  1. University of Utah Department of Human Genetics Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion committee
  2. University of Montana SACNAS Chapter
  3. We Are Montana in the Classroom
  4. SpectrUM
  5. University of Montana Women in Science
  6. USC Science Outreach (SCout)
